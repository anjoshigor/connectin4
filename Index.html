1 - Rules

Connectin'4 is a online version of the famous game Connect 4. Tha main objectitve of the player must be to connect 4 pieces straight in line. It consists in 2 players(Player vs CPU or Player vs Player) in competition to see who will connect the 4 pieces first, each player plays with pieces whose has different colors. In a future version the players will be capable to choose the color of their pieces.

3 - Options and preferences

The player can set preferences like colors of the pieces in the options section - still in development!!!
